# Claim Adjudicated Event

## Overview

The claim adjudicated event is emitted by the Rules Engine when it completes the adjudication process and determines a claim disposition. This event carries the adjudication result and triggers downstream payment or denial processing.

## Details

Generated by the Rules Engine after evaluating a claim against benefit plan terms, provider contracts, medical policies, and authorization status. The event payload includes the claim disposition (PAY, DENY, or PEND), calculated allowed amount, member cost-sharing amounts, and any applicable reason codes. PAY dispositions trigger the Payment Engine, DENY dispositions trigger EOB generation with denial reasons, and PEND dispositions route claims to manual review queues.
