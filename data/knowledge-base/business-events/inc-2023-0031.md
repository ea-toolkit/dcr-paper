# INC-2023-0031: Payment File Corruption Incident

## Overview

On April 28, 2023, a race condition between concurrent void/reissue operations and the nightly payment batch job caused duplicate payment records for 147 provider TINs to be included in the NACHA EFT file. The duplicate payments totaling $2.3M were processed by the banking partner before detection, requiring manual recoupment efforts over 4 months.

## Details

The incident was triggered when Claims Ops initiated bulk reprocessing of 3,200 claims for a Statewide Physicians Group fee schedule correction, generating ~800 void/reissue operations concurrent with the nightly payment cycle. The payment batching query lacked proper transaction isolation, picking up both voided payments (not yet committed) and their replacements. Impact included $2.3M in duplicate payments to 147 TINs, only $412K recoverable via banking partner reversals, remainder requiring manual recoupment through August 2023. Seven provider offices complained about recoupment letters, and reputational damage occurred with Statewide Physicians Group.
