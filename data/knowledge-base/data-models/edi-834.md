# EDI 834

## Overview

The standardized EDI transaction format used to communicate member enrollment information between health plans, employers, and insurance brokers. EDI 834 files contain member demographic information, coverage details, and enrollment changes.

## Details

EDI 834 is the Health Insurance Benefit Enrollment and Maintenance transaction standard that carries member enrollment data in a structured electronic format. These files contain member demographics (names, addresses, dates of birth), coverage effective dates, benefit plan selections, dependent information, and enrollment change indicators. The Eligibility Service processes these files as part of the technical implementation of enrollment workflows. The format includes specific segments and data elements that must be parsed and validated during processing.
