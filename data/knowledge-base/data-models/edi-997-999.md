# EDI 997/999

## Overview

Standard EDI transaction sets (997/999) generated by Claims Gateway to acknowledge receipt and processing status of inbound EDI documents from trading partners.

## Details

EDI 997 (Functional Acknowledgment) and 999 (Implementation Acknowledgment) transaction sets provide automated confirmation of EDI document receipt and processing results. Generated by Claims Gateway as the final step in EDI transaction processing workflow and transmitted back to trading partners via SFTP or AS2.
