# ERA 835

## Overview

ERA 835 is the standard electronic format for communicating payment information to healthcare providers. It provides detailed remittance advice showing what was paid, denied, or adjusted for each claim, replacing paper explanation of payment documents.

## Details

Standard EDI transaction format generated by the Payment Engine to inform providers about claim payments and adjustments. Contains payment amounts, denial reasons, adjustment codes, and detailed claim-level information. Provides electronic remittance advice that providers can automatically import into their practice management systems. Generated as part of the payment cycle processing and made available through the Payment API. This electronic format improves provider reconciliation and reduces manual payment posting.
