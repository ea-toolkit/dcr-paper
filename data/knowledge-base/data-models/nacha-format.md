# NACHA Format

## Overview

Industry-standard format for electronic payment files generated by Payment Engine and sent to banking partners. Contains payment instructions for provider EFT payments and member reimbursements.

## Details

National Automated Clearing House Association (NACHA) standard format used for all electronic payment transactions. Payment Engine generates these files nightly during payment cycle processing and transmits them via SFTP to the banking partner for settlement.
