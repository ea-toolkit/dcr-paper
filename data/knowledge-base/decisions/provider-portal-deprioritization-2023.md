# Provider Portal Deprioritization (2023)

## Overview

Strategic decision made in December 2023 to deprioritize the Provider Portal project in favor of the Rules Engine migration. The project was originally planned for 2024 delivery but was paused with potential restart in Q1 2026.

## Details

The Provider Portal project was paused in December 2023 despite having requirements documented and timeline planned for Q1-Q4 2024 delivery. The decision was made to prioritize the Rules Engine migration project over provider portal development. As of October 2025, the project is tentatively scheduled for Q1 2026 roadmap, with James re-validating requirements. This decision reflects the strategic importance of the Rules Engine migration and limited development resources.
