# Q3 2025 Performance Metrics

## Overview

Set of key performance indicators tracking claims processing efficiency, accuracy, and throughput. Shows 81.3% auto-adjudication rate against 85% target, 4.2 second average processing time, and 99.7% payment accuracy.

## Details

Claims volume reached 2.4M (up 8% from Q2) split between Professional (2.0M), Institutional (350K), and Pharmacy (50K on legacy system). Auto-adjudication rate of 81.3% remains below 85% target due to incomplete rules migration from HealthLogic. Average processing time improved from 5.1 to 4.2 seconds due to synchronous fraud scoring. Denial rate of 12.4% with top reasons being member eligibility issues (3.2%), missing authorizations (2.8%), duplicates (2.1%), non-covered services (1.9%), and timely filing limits (1.4%). Payment accuracy at 99.7% with fraud holds affecting 0.05% of volume (1,247 claims).
