# SIU Investigation Targets

## Overview

Service level agreement requiring Special Investigation Unit to investigate 95% of fraud-held claims within 10 business days. Q3 2025 performance was 89% due to staffing shortages.

## Details

Performance target and service level agreement requiring the Special Investigation Unit (SIU) to investigate 95% of fraud-held claims within 10 business days of the hold being placed. In Q3 2025, actual performance was 89% (investigating 89% of 1,247 fraud-held claims within target), falling short due to SIU staffing shortages when two investigators left in August without replacement. The shortfall creates risk of investigation backlog growth.
