# Banking Partner

## Overview

Banking partner handling electronic funds transfers and ACH payments generated by Payment Engine. Receives NACHA-formatted files via SFTP and provides daily settlement confirmations.

## Details

Receives nightly NACHA-formatted EFT/ACH files from Payment Engine via SFTP for payment processing. Provides daily settlement confirmation files showing payment status. Critical partner for the payment cycle workflow enabling provider and member reimbursements.
