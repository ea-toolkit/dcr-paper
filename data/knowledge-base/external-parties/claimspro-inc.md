# ClaimsPro Inc.

## Overview

ClaimsPro Inc. was the vendor that provided the ClaimsPro monolithic claims processing system to Clearview. By 2020, they had effectively stopped development on the product, contributing to Clearview's decision to migrate away from the system.

## Details

ClaimsPro Inc. was the software vendor that developed and maintained the ClaimsPro system that Clearview used from 2014 to 2021. The company's lack of continued development and support for the product was one of the key factors driving Clearview's migration to a microservices architecture. Their abandonment of the product left Clearview with a legacy system that couldn't scale to meet growing business needs.
