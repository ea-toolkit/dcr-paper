# Clearview Health Plans

## Overview

Clearview is the primary organization operating the claims processing business. It provides health insurance coverage to approximately 800,000 members across three states.

## Details

Clearview Health Plans is a regional health insurance company that processes claims through a pipeline where providers render services to members, submit claims, and Clearview determines coverage under member plans before paying providers or denying claims.
