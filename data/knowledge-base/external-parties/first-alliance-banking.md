# First Alliance Banking

## Overview

First Alliance Banking serves as Clearview's banking partner for EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) processing. They handle provider payment transactions and provide support for urgent payment reversals.

## Details

First Alliance Banking is Clearview's banking partner responsible for EFT processing, which is critical for provider payments in the claims workflow. They provide support through a shared inbox (payments-support@firstalliancebanking.com) and have a dedicated commercial payments desk for urgent payment reversals accessible at (800) 555-1FAB. They also provide SFTP support through sftp-support@firstalliancebanking.com. This banking relationship is essential for the Payment Engine's ability to process provider payments efficiently.
