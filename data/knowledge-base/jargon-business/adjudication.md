# Adjudication

## Overview

Adjudication is the core business process where submitted claims are evaluated against benefit plan rules, medical policies, and other criteria to determine the appropriate disposition.

## Details

This term refers to the systematic evaluation of healthcare claims against member benefit plans, fee schedules, medical policy edits, and other business rules to make a determination of whether to pay the claim, deny it, or hold it for manual review (pend). This is the fundamental decision-making process in claims processing.
