# Allowed amount

## Overview

Allowed amount is the maximum reimbursement amount that the health plan will pay for a covered service, determined by the contracted rate with the provider or fee schedule.

## Details

The allowed amount represents the ceiling for payment that the health plan has established for specific healthcare services, typically based on negotiated contracts with in-network providers or established fee schedules for out-of-network services. This amount determines the basis for member cost-sharing calculations such as coinsurance and deductibles.
