# Capitation

## Overview

Alternative payment model where providers receive fixed monthly payments per enrolled member rather than fee-for-service payments. Currently not supported by Clearview's Payment Engine but requested by larger provider groups in contract negotiations.

## Details

Flat per-member-per-month (PMPM) payment structure that contrasts with Clearview's current fee-for-service only model. Large multi-specialty provider groups are requesting capitated contracts during negotiations. Implementation would require significant changes to the Payment Engine architecture. Provider Network team and larger provider groups have been advocating for this capability, leading to plans for a Q1 2026 design proposal to scope the effort.
