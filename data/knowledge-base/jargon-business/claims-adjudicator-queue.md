# Claims Adjudicator Queue

## Overview

Work management system where claims requiring manual review (PEND disposition) are queued for evaluation by experienced claims adjudicators who can make final pay/deny determinations.

## Details

When the automated rules engine cannot determine a clear PAY or DENY disposition for a claim, it assigns a PEND status and routes the claim to the claims adjudicator queue. This queue is staffed by senior claims personnel who have the expertise and authority to review complex scenarios, interpret edge cases in benefit language, evaluate medical necessity, and make final adjudication decisions. The manual review process ensures that claims too complex for automated processing still receive appropriate evaluation rather than automatic denial.
