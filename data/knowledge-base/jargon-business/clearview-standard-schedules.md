# Clearview Standard Schedules

## Overview

Fallback pricing schedules developed and maintained by Clearview's actuarial team for services where neither contracted rates nor usual-and-customary rates are available.

## Details

Clearview standard schedules represent internal pricing tables maintained by the actuarial team to handle edge cases where neither provider contract rates nor usual-and-customary rates are available for determining allowed amounts. These schedules ensure that every claim can receive appropriate pricing for benefit calculation purposes. The actuarial team's involvement suggests these schedules are based on statistical analysis and risk management principles rather than arbitrary pricing decisions.
