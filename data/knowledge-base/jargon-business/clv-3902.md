# CLV-3902

## Overview

CLV-3902 was an action item to implement a 2,000 claim limit on the /claims/batch endpoint to prevent resource exhaustion from overly large batch submissions.

## Details

CLV-3902 addressed the need for admission control on batch size following the March 2024 incident where a 14,000 claim batch overwhelmed the system. The action item added validation to the Claims Submission API's /claims/batch endpoint to reject batches larger than 2,000 claims. This forces large submissions to be split into manageable chunks. The work was completed and merged on March 25, 2024, making it one of the first remediation items completed after the incident.
