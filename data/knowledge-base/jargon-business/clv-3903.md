# CLV-3903

## Overview

CLV-3903 refers to the March 12, 2024 connection pool exhaustion incident that led to significant changes in Claims Gateway monitoring and database connectivity management.

## Details

CLV-3903 represents a critical incident from March 12, 2024 involving connection pool exhaustion in the Claims Gateway system. This incident was significant enough to drive architectural changes including the addition of HikariCP pool utilization monitoring with 90% thresholds and 2-minute alert windows. The incident also influenced the subsequent PgBouncer migration in Q3 2024 and the current 200 connection maximum configuration. The incident identifier follows Clearview's CLV-#### naming convention for tracking operational issues and postmortems.
