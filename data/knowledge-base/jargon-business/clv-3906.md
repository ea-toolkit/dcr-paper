# CLV-3906

## Overview

CLV-3906 was a communication action item to inform Northeast Clearinghouse about the new 2,000 claim batch size limit and help them adjust their submission process.

## Details

CLV-3906 involved reaching out to Northeast Clearinghouse to notify them about the new batch size limits implemented after their 14,000 claim submission caused the March 2024 outage. James Whitfield was responsible for this communication. The clearinghouse was informed about the 2,000 claim limit and adjusted their submission process accordingly to comply with the new restrictions. This demonstrates the business impact of technical decisions and the need to coordinate with external partners when system constraints change.
