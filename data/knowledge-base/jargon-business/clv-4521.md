# CLV-4521

## Overview

Known technical debt issue where accumulator reservations need manual cleanup when the Rules Engine crashes mid-adjudication. Currently handled by batch job cleanup every 6 hours for reservations older than 4 hours.

## Details

JIRA ticket documenting a workaround in the accumulator reservation system. When the Rules Engine crashes during claim adjudication, soft reservations on member benefit accumulators may not be properly released, potentially causing incorrect benefit calculations. The current solution is a batch cleanup job that expires reservations older than 4 hours, running every 6 hours. This represents known technical debt in the concurrency control system.
