# CLV-5102

## Overview

Tracking number for the issue where archive retention is set to 7 years per state regulation but the GCS lifecycle policy is configured for only 5 years. Filed as an issue but not prioritized for resolution.

## Details

Represents a compliance risk where scanned claim images in gs://clearview-claims-scans/ bucket have a 2-year gap between the required 7-year retention period and the actual 5-year GCS lifecycle policy configuration. This could result in premature deletion of records that must be retained for regulatory compliance.
