# COB

## Overview

COB (Coordination of Benefits) refers to the process of determining payment responsibility when a health plan member has coverage from multiple insurance sources.

## Details

Coordination of Benefits is the business process and set of rules that determines which insurance plan pays primary, secondary, or tertiary when a member has coverage from multiple payers such as employer plans, spouse plans, or government programs. This ensures proper payment coordination and prevents overpayment.
