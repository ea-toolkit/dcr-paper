# EOB

## Overview

EOB (Explanation of Benefits) is the document sent to health plan members that details what services were covered, amounts paid, and member responsibility for processed claims.

## Details

An Explanation of Benefits is a formal document provided to health plan members that breaks down the coverage determination for their submitted claims, including what services were covered by the plan, amounts paid to providers, and any remaining member financial responsibility such as copayments or deductibles.
