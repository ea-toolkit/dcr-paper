# Fee-for-Service

## Overview

Clearview's current payment model where providers receive payment based on services rendered rather than flat monthly amounts. Contrasts with capitation model being requested by provider groups.

## Details

The existing payment model used by Clearview's Payment Engine where providers are compensated for each individual service, procedure, or claim processed. This model ties payment directly to utilization and services provided, unlike capitation which provides fixed monthly payments regardless of services rendered. All current provider contracts operate under this model.
