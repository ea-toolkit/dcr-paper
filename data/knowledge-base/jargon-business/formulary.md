# Formulary

## Overview

Health plan-specific list defining which prescription medications are covered under different benefit plans. Currently managed by the legacy pharmacy system and not integrated with Clearview's main platform.

## Details

The comprehensive list of prescription drugs covered under each health plan's pharmacy benefits. Each plan may have different formularies with varying coverage levels, copays, and restrictions. Currently managed entirely within the legacy pharmacy system (PharmaCore) and represents a significant integration challenge when pharmacy claims are migrated to Clearview's main processing pipeline. The lack of integration means formulary data is not available to the main claims processing system.
