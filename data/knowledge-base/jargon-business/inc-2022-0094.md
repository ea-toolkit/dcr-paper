# INC-2022-0094

## Overview

The formal incident tracking number assigned to the September 17, 2022 Eligibility Service outage. This SEV-1 incident lasted 3 hours 47 minutes and generated multiple follow-up action items tracked with sub-identifiers like INC-2022-0094-A1 through INC-2022-0094-A4.

## Details

INC-2022-0094 is the incident management system identifier for the database migration outage that impacted the Eligibility Service, Claims Gateway, Rules Engine, and Member Portal. The incident generated four specific action items: A1 (DBA review requirement - completed), A2 (PostgreSQL best practices documentation - completed), A3 (CI pre-deploy checks - completed), and A4 (circuit breaker implementation - not started). Action item A4 was later reopened as CLV-3904 after the March 2024 gateway outage, showing the ongoing relevance of this incident's lessons.
