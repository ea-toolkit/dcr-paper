# INC-2023-0031

## Overview

Internal incident tracking identifier for the April 2023 payment file corruption incident where duplicate provider payments totaling $2.3M were processed due to race condition in payment batching logic.

## Details

Standard incident numbering format following pattern INC-YYYY-NNNN where YYYY is year and NNNN is sequential number. This particular incident was significant enough to generate multiple follow-up action items (A1 through A5) and took over 2 years to fully resolve all remediation actions.
