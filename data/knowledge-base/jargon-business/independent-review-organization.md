# Independent Review Organization

## Overview

Third-party medical review organization that provides independent evaluation of disputed claim decisions in Level 2 appeals, with binding authority over Clearview's coverage determinations.

## Details

Independent Review Organizations (IROs) are external entities that provide Level 2 appeals review when members remain unsatisfied after internal appeal decisions. IROs conduct independent clinical and administrative reviews of disputed cases, bringing objectivity to coverage determinations. Their decisions are binding on Clearview, providing members with an external recourse mechanism for coverage disputes. This external review process is typically required by state insurance regulations to ensure fair resolution of claim disputes.
