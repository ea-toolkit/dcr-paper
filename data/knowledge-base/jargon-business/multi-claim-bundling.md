# Multi-Claim Bundling

## Overview

Complex business rules that identify and group related claims that should be processed together rather than independently. Part of the challenging rules being migrated from HealthLogic system.

## Details

Sophisticated adjudication logic that identifies claims that should be grouped and processed together for accurate payment determination. This includes scenarios like multiple claims from the same episode of care, related surgical procedures, or claims that need coordinated benefit application. These bundling rules are among the complex logic being migrated from HealthLogic and contribute to the difficulty in achieving the 85% auto-adjudication target.
