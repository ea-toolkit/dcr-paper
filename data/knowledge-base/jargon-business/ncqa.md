# NCQA

## Overview

Healthcare quality organization that sets standards for provider credentialing cycles. Referenced in the context of the 3-year provider re-credentialing requirement that drives credential expiry tracking in the Provider Directory.

## Details

National Committee for Quality Assurance is a healthcare accreditation organization that establishes standards for healthcare quality and provider credentialing. In the Clearview system, NCQA standards drive the 3-year re-credentialing cycle requirement that necessitates tracking credential expiry dates and credentialing status (ACTIVE/PENDING/EXPIRED/REVOKED) in the provider data model.
