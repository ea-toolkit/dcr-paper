# NPI

## Overview

NPI (National Provider Identifier) is the standardized unique identifier assigned to healthcare providers for identification in healthcare transactions and data exchange.

## Details

The National Provider Identifier is a federal standard for uniquely identifying healthcare providers in electronic transactions. Each healthcare provider (individual practitioners and organizations) receives a unique 10-digit NPI number that is used consistently across all healthcare transactions and administrative systems.
