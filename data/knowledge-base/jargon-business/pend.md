# Pend

## Overview

Pend is the disposition assigned to claims that cannot be automatically processed and require manual review by claims examiners or other specialists before a final pay or deny decision can be made.

## Details

When a claim is marked as 'pend', it means the automated adjudication system has identified conditions that prevent automatic processing, such as missing information, policy exceptions, or complex medical scenarios that require human expertise. These claims are routed to appropriate staff for manual review and decision-making.
