# PMPM

## Overview

Healthcare industry acronym for Per-Member-Per-Month, representing the fixed monthly payment amount per enrolled member in capitation contracts. Standard metric for capitated payment arrangements.

## Details

Industry-standard acronym meaning Per-Member-Per-Month, used to express the fixed monthly payment rate in capitation contracts. Represents the amount a provider receives each month for each enrolled member regardless of services provided to that member. Key metric for capitation payment models that Clearview is considering supporting.
