# TOB

## Overview

Standardized code found in Field 4 of UB-04 forms that indicates the type of billing being submitted by institutional providers such as hospitals and facilities.

## Details

Field extracted by OCR pipeline from UB-04 institutional claim forms as part of the structured data capture process. Used to categorize different types of institutional billing scenarios and processing requirements.
