# Usual and Customary Rate

## Overview

Rate determination method used for out-of-network providers when no contracted fee schedule exists. Based on typical charges for similar services in the same geographic area.

## Details

When processing claims from out-of-network providers, the usual and customary (U&C) rate serves as the allowed amount for benefit calculations. This rate represents what providers in the same geographic area typically charge for similar services, providing a fair market-based reference point. U&C rates protect members from excessive out-of-network charges while ensuring reasonable provider compensation. The rate is used instead of contracted fee schedules (which don't exist for non-contracted providers) to determine the baseline for member cost-sharing calculations.
