# Clearview Provider Relations Rep

## Overview

Internal Clearview employee responsible for reviewing provider-submitted changes and acting as a gatekeeper for sensitive modifications. This role ensures data quality and prevents unauthorized changes to critical provider information.

## Details

Provider Relations Representatives are internal Clearview users who would review and approve provider-submitted changes through the portal, particularly for sensitive data like address and phone number updates. They serve as gatekeepers for changes that could impact provider payments or network participation. The requirement specifies they should review changes within 2 business days. They would have administrative access to approve or reject provider submissions.
