# Credentialing Specialist

## Overview

Internal Clearview employee specialized in credentialing processes who reviews provider-uploaded documents and manages the re-credentialing workflow. This role ensures providers maintain proper credentials and comply with NCQA requirements.

## Details

Credentialing Specialists are internal users who would be notified when providers upload new credentialing documents through the portal. They review medical licenses, DEA certificates, malpractice insurance, board certifications, and CME records to ensure provider compliance. They manage the re-credentialing workflow and monitor credential expiration dates, with system alerts sent 90 days before expiration per NCQA requirements for 3-year re-credentialing cycles.
