# Dana Okafor

## Overview

Dana Okafor is the team lead for Member Services, the team responsible for member-facing systems including the Eligibility Service and Member Portal. Her team focuses on member experience and data management.

## Details

Named as the Member Services team lead responsible for two key systems: Eligibility Service (which handles member enrollment, EDI 270/271 transactions, and accumulator tracking) and Member Portal (the consumer-facing application for members to view claims, benefits, and submit OON claims). Her team's focus is on member experience and the foundational eligibility data that drives the entire claims processing pipeline.
