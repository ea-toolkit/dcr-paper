# Member Services Call Center Supervisor

## Overview

Supervisory role within the Member Services call center responsible for handling escalated member complaints, particularly those related to eligibility and accumulator calculation issues.

## Details

Final escalation point for member escalations related to eligibility service issues. Handles complex member complaints that cannot be resolved by front-line call center staff, particularly those involving accumulator calculation disputes, benefit determination issues, and cases requiring manual intervention or adjustments.
