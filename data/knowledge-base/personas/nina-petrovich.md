# Nina Petrovich

## Overview

Nina Petrovich was a Senior Engineer who led the rewrite of the Payment Engine during the ClaimsPro migration. She designed the void/reissue workflow and left Clearview in Q4 2022.

## Details

Nina Petrovich played a critical role in the ClaimsPro migration by leading the Payment Engine extraction and rewrite. The payment module was identified as the most challenging extraction due to its deep entanglement with ClaimsPro's adjudication logic, requiring a complete rewrite rather than simple extraction. She designed the void/reissue workflow that is still used today. Her departure in Q4 2022 coincided with the completion of the major migration work and the team reorganization period.
