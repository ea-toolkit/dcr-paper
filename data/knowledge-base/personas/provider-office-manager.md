# Provider Office Manager

## Overview

The main user of the Provider Portal who manages credentials, updates practice information, and checks payment status. This persona represents the administrative staff at healthcare provider offices who handle the business operations and interact with health plans on behalf of the providers.

## Details

Provider Office Managers are the primary target users for the Provider Portal, responsible for managing provider demographic information, uploading credentialing documents, monitoring network participation status, accessing fee schedules, and tracking payment history. They handle the day-to-day administrative interactions with Clearview and would be the most frequent users of the portal's functionality. In the current manual process, they email or fax changes to the Provider Network team.
