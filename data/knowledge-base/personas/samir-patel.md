# Samir Patel

## Overview

Samir Patel was the Principal Architect who led the migration from ClaimsPro to microservices architecture in 2020-2021. He authored this architectural documentation and recommended splitting the Claims Platform team into three teams before leaving in Q2 2022.

## Details

Samir Patel served as Principal Architect and was the primary driver of the ClaimsPro migration project. He led the architectural decisions including the microservices decomposition strategy and vendor evaluations. He created this architectural documentation and was responsible for the overall technical direction during the critical migration period. Before leaving Clearview in Q2 2022, he recommended the organizational restructuring that split the single Claims Platform team into the current three teams (Claims Ops, Member Services, Provider Network). His departure appears to have coincided with the completion of the initial migration phase.
