# Tom Nguyen

## Overview

Tom Nguyen was a Senior Engineer on the original Claims Platform team during the ClaimsPro migration. He left Clearview in Q1 2023.

## Details

Tom Nguyen served as a Senior Engineer on the unified Claims Platform team during the 2021 migration period. He was part of the core engineering team that executed the migration from ClaimsPro to the microservices architecture. He left the company in Q1 2023, which was after the team reorganization into separate Claims Ops, Member Services, and Provider Network teams.
