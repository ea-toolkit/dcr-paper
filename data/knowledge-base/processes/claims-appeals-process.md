# Claims Appeals Process

## Overview

Formal appeals workflow allowing members and providers to challenge denied or underpaid claims through Level 1 internal review and Level 2 external review by Independent Review Organizations.

## Details

The appeals process provides two levels of review for disputed claim decisions. Level 1 is internal appeal handled by Claims Operations, reviewed by senior claims adjudicators with decisions required within 30 days (72 hours for urgent/pre-service appeals). If the internal appeal is upheld and the member remains unsatisfied, they can request Level 2 external review by an Independent Review Organization (IRO). The IRO provides an independent clinical and administrative review, and their decision is binding on Clearview. Appeals are tracked in a separate workflow system but reference the original claim_id to maintain audit trails and ensure proper case management.
