# COBRA Enrollment Process

## Overview

The specialized enrollment workflow that handles members who have lost employer-sponsored health coverage and are eligible for COBRA continuation benefits. This involves different rules, timelines, and premium structures than standard enrollment.

## Details

COBRA (Consolidated Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act) enrollment is a federally mandated process that allows employees and their dependents to continue group health coverage after losing employer coverage due to job loss, reduction in hours, or other qualifying events. This process has specific legal requirements for notification timelines, coverage continuation periods, and premium payment structures. The workflow must handle the transition from employer-paid to member-paid premiums and manage the different benefit structures that may apply to COBRA coverage.
