# Member Portal Redesign

## Overview

Major redesign of the Member Portal launching in November 2025 as part of open enrollment preparation. Features new claims status page and improved benefit balance display for better member experience.

## Details

Comprehensive redesign of the Member Portal scheduled for November 2025 launch, timed to coincide with open enrollment period. Key improvements include a new claims status page that provides better visibility into claim processing status and an improved benefit balance display that makes it easier for members to understand their deductible progress, out-of-pocket maximums, and remaining benefits. This redesign is part of the broader open enrollment preparation effort.
