# On-Call Handoff Process

## Overview

The on-call handoff process occurs every Monday at 9 AM ET in the #claims-on-call Slack channel. The outgoing engineer posts a summary of anything in progress to ensure continuity.

## Details

The on-call handoff process ensures smooth transition of on-call responsibilities within the Claims Operations team. It happens weekly on Mondays at 9 AM Eastern Time in the #claims-on-call Slack channel. The outgoing on-call engineer is responsible for posting a summary of any ongoing issues, incidents, or work in progress to the incoming engineer. This process helps maintain continuity of operations and ensures no critical issues are lost during the transition. The PagerDuty schedule manages the formal on-call rotation.
