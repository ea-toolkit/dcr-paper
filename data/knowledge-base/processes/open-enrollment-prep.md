# Open Enrollment Preparation

## Overview

Comprehensive preparation process for the annual open enrollment period when members can change health plans and benefits. Involves system scaling, portal updates, and eligibility service readiness.

## Details

Annual operational preparation for open enrollment period starting November 1, 2025. Includes Member Portal redesign with new claims status page and improved benefit balance display launching in November, Eligibility Service scaling plan to handle enrollment spike, and coordination across teams to ensure system readiness for increased member activity and plan changes. Dana Okafor leads this effort for Member Services team.
