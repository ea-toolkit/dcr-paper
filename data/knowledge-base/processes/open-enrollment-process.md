# Open Enrollment Process

## Overview

The workflow that manages the annual open enrollment period when existing members can modify their health plan selections, change coverage levels, or switch between available plan options. This involves plan comparison, selection validation, and effective date management.

## Details

A critical enrollment scenario that occurs annually, allowing existing members to review and change their health plan coverage. This process would involve presenting available plan options, validating member selections, managing effective dates for changes, and coordinating with benefit plan systems to ensure smooth transitions. The process likely has specific timing windows and business rules around what changes are permitted during open enrollment.
