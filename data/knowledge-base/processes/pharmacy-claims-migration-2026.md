# Pharmacy Claims Migration (2026)

## Overview

A planned initiative for 2026 to bring pharmacy claims processing onto Clearview's main claims platform. This migration will require integrating NCPDP standards, formulary systems, PBM relationships, pharmacy networks, and clinical review processes.

## Details

The 2026 roadmap includes migrating pharmacy claims from the legacy PharmaCore system to the main Clearview platform. Key integration points include: NCPDP transaction standards (pharmacy equivalent of EDI 837), formulary integration for covered drug lists per benefit plan, PBM integration decisions (continue external or bring in-house), pharmacy network and pricing integration (MAC, AWP, WAC pricing models), and clinical drug review/DUR integration. Priya Anand is designated as the engineering contact for migration planning.
