# Provider Portal Design

## Overview

New provider-facing portal in early design phase to enable providers to self-service credential updates rather than requiring manual Provider Network team intervention.

## Details

Provider-facing portal project in early design phase that would allow providers to update their own credentials, demographics, and other directory information through self-service rather than requiring Provider Network team manual processing. This would reduce administrative burden on the Provider Network team and provide faster updates for providers. Currently in early design phase with no committed timeline.
