# Qualifying Life Events Process

## Overview

The workflow that manages enrollment changes outside of open enrollment periods when members experience qualifying life events such as marriage, birth of a child, or job changes. These events create special enrollment periods allowing plan modifications.

## Details

This process handles specific life events that qualify members for special enrollment periods: marriage (which may add dependents or change coverage needs), birth of children (requiring dependent enrollment), and job changes (which may affect eligibility or plan options). The process must validate that the life event qualifies for enrollment changes, manage timing requirements for when changes can be made, and coordinate effective dates with the triggering event dates.
