# Quarterly Operations Review

## Overview

Standard quarterly review process where teams present performance metrics, discuss progress on key initiatives, and align on upcoming priorities. Attended by leadership from Claims Ops, Member Services, Provider Network, and Fraud Detection teams.

## Details

Structured quarterly meeting format covering claims processing metrics (volume, auto-adjudication rates, processing times, denial rates, payment accuracy), fraud detection performance, team updates on major initiatives, incident reviews, and priority setting for the upcoming quarter. The Q3 2025 review showed 2.4M claims processed with 81.3% auto-adjudication rate, ongoing rules engine migration challenges, and preparation for open enrollment period.
