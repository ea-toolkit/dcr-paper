# SIU Review Process

## Overview

Manual review workflow for claims scoring above 0.82 fraud threshold. SIU investigators must clear or formally investigate before claims can proceed to adjudication.

## Details

When claims score above the 0.82 fraud threshold, they are automatically held and routed to the Special Investigations Unit (SIU) for manual review. These claims cannot proceed to adjudication until SIU takes action. SIU investigators can either: 1) Clear the claim for normal processing if the high score was a false positive, or 2) Open a formal fraud investigation if suspicious activity is confirmed. This process acts as a critical control point to prevent payment of potentially fraudulent claims before they enter the adjudication pipeline.
