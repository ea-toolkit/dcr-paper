# Claims War Room Zoom

## Overview

A permanent Zoom meeting room dedicated to Claims Operations incident response. The room is always active during incidents and used for war room coordination during SEV-1 incidents.

## Details

The Claims War Room Zoom is a permanent meeting room (https://zoom.us/j/clearview-claims-warroom) dedicated to incident response coordination. It remains always active during incidents and is specifically used when SEV-1 incidents require war room activation after 30 minutes without resolution. This provides a dedicated communication channel for real-time collaboration during critical incidents affecting claims processing systems.
