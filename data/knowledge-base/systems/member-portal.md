# Member Portal

## Overview

Member Portal is the consumer-facing application that provides members with access to claims status, benefit balances, provider search functionality, EOBs, and digital ID cards.

## Details

This system serves as the primary digital interface for health plan members, offering self-service capabilities including claims status checking, benefit balance viewing, provider directory search, access to Explanation of Benefits (EOB) documents, and digital insurance ID cards. It's owned and maintained by the Member Services team.
