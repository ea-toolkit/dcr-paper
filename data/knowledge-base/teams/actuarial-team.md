# Actuarial Team

## Overview

Specialized team that develops and maintains internal pricing schedules and performs statistical risk analysis to support claims processing and benefit design.

## Details

The actuarial team maintains Clearview standard schedules used as fallback pricing when contracted rates or usual-and-customary rates aren't available. This team applies statistical analysis, risk management principles, and actuarial science to develop appropriate pricing tables. Their involvement in fee schedule maintenance suggests they also support broader financial and risk management functions for the organization.
