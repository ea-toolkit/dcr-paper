# Benefits Administration Team

## Overview

Non-engineering team that manages the benefits administration system and enrollment data processing. Separate organizational unit from the engineering teams but critical dependency for eligibility operations.

## Details

HR/Benefits team responsible for managing the benefits administration system that provides enrollment data to Clearview engineering systems. Contact point for enrollment data issues that originate from the upstream benefits administration system. Operates independently from engineering teams but provides critical dependency for Member Services and Eligibility Service operations.
