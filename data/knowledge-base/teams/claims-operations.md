# Claims Operations

## Overview

Claims Operations is responsible for the complete claims processing pipeline from intake through payment, including the gateway, rules engine, payment engine, fraud detection, and pre-authorization systems.

## Details

This team has end-to-end ownership of the claims processing workflow and all associated systems including Claims Gateway, Rules Engine, Payment Engine, Fraud Detection, and Pre-Auth Service. They are responsible for the operational aspects of processing claims from submission through final disposition.
