# Claims Platform Team (2021)

## Overview

The Claims Platform team was the single engineering team responsible for the entire claims processing platform during the 2021 migration period. It was later split into three specialized teams in late 2022 as the platform grew in complexity.

## Details

The Claims Platform team was formed to execute the ClaimsPro migration and initially owned all claims processing systems. The team included Samir Patel (Principal Architect), Marcus Reeves (Engineering Manager), Tom Nguyen and Nina Petrovich (Senior Engineers), Dana Okafor, James Whitfield, and later Priya Anand (mid-2021). The team was responsible for extracting modules from ClaimsPro, building new systems like Claims Gateway, and managing the entire migration process. In late 2022, following Samir's recommendation, the team was split into three specialized teams: Claims Operations (led by Marcus), Member Services (led by Dana), and Provider Network (led by James). This reorganization reflected the platform's growth and the need for more focused domain expertise.
