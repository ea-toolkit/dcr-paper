## Overview

Specialized team that handles coordination of benefits policy questions and broader regulatory compliance issues for Clearview Health Plans operations.

Subject matter experts for COB policy interpretation and regulatory compliance. Contact point for complex COB policy questions that arise during eligibility operations or member escalations. Separate from engineering teams but provides critical policy guidance for proper COB handling and compliance with healthcare regulations.
