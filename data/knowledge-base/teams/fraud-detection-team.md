# Fraud Detection Team

## Overview

Specialized team that develops and maintains fraud detection algorithms, manages model retraining cycles, and performs post-payment fraud analysis.

## Details

The fraud detection team manages the ML models that score claims for fraud risk, handles the monthly retraining cycles to adapt to new fraud patterns, and conducts post-payment analysis of flagged claims. Team member Tariq Hassan serves as a subject matter expert for fraud model questions. The team is responsible for both pre-payment scoring (which can hold claims for SIU review) and post-payment pattern analysis that identifies broader fraud schemes.
