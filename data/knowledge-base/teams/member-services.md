# Member Services

## Overview

Member Services team owns and operates the member-facing systems including the Member Portal and Eligibility Service, focusing on member experience and eligibility management.

## Details

This team is responsible for systems that directly serve health plan members, including the consumer-facing Member Portal application and the Eligibility Service that manages member enrollment and benefit tracking. They focus on member experience and eligibility verification capabilities.
